Overview:

The Department of Computer Science was established as an independent entity in the academic year 2000-2001. Since then, it has been dedicated to advancing technical education and scientific research in the rapidly evolving fields of computer science and its applications. As an autonomous department under Government Holkar (Model Autonomous) Science College, Indore (M.P.), affiliated with DAVV, Indore, our mission is to nurture skilled computer science specialists ready to tackle global challenges.

We offer a diverse range of courses including BCA, B.Sc. Computer Science, M.Sc. Computer Science, and PGDCA, all designed to reflect our commitment to excellence. Our graduates are highly sought after and have gone on to join leading companies worldwide. We emphasize the development of analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and effective communication skills. Our rigorous curriculum balances theory with hands-on practical experience, with over 100 students actively engaged in voluntary software development projects for both government and non-government organizations.

Empowering students with knowledge, skills, and innovation to shape the future of technology.
